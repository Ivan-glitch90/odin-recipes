Lasagna

Description:

This are the steps and ingredients list to make a vegan lasagna

Ingredients:

  • Vegan Cheese
  • Vegan meat
  • Pasta (Of course!)
  • Veggies

Steps:

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Cook the meat
  3. Put a layer of pasta followed by a layer of meat and cheese, repeat until container is full
  4. Cook in oven at 350F for 45 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
